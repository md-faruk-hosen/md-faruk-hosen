## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am **Md. Faruk Hosen**, currently serving as a **Lecturer** in the **Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)** at **Begum Rokeya University, Rangpur, Bangladesh**.

Previously, I worked as:

- 🎓 **Lecturer**, Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Netrokona University
- 🎓 **Senior Lecturer**, Department of Computing and Information Systems (CIS), Daffodil International University

I received both my **M.Sc. (Engineering)** and **B.Sc. (Engineering)** degrees in **Information and Communication Technology (ICT)** from **Mawlana Bhashani Science and Technology University (MBSTU)**. I graduated **first in my class** in both degree programs and was awarded **Gold Medals** for securing the highest position in the Faculty of Engineering.

My research focuses on developing **Artificial Intelligence-driven computational methods** for solving complex biomedical and healthcare problems through **Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Bioinformatics, and Computational Drug Discovery**.

I have authored **40+ peer-reviewed publications** in internationally recognized journals and conferences published by **IEEE, Elsevier, Springer**, and other **Scopus-indexed venues**.

I am passionate about interdisciplinary research, innovation, and collaborative problem-solving. I am currently seeking opportunities to pursue a **fully funded Ph.D.** while actively exploring international research collaborations.
